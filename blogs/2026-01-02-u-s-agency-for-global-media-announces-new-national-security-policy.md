U.S. Agency for Global Media announces new National Security policy. Effective December 23, 2025, all USAGM personnel should be familiar with this Strategy and use it, as appropriate, as a framework for programming, consistent with the broadcasting standards and principles set forth in the International Broadcasting Act.
